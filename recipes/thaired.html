Spicy Thai Red Curry

Description

A REAL CROWD PLEASER 'GAENG PED', OUR CLASSIC RED CURRY MADE FROM DRIED RED CHILLIES BLENDED WITH COCONUT MILK.

Main ingredients/h5>
  • 50g Red curry paste
  • 200g Chickens
  • 450 ml. Coconut milk
  • 4 Red Thai Chillies
  • 2 Tsp palm Sugar
  • 10 Basil Leaves
  • 2 Tbsp vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Put a medium sized pan on a medium heat
  2. Add the oil and curry paste, stir it until fragrant.
  3. Reduce the heat, gradually add a little coconut milk at a time and stir until a film of red oil surfaces.
  4. When the curry is boiling, add the chicken and kaffir lime leafs.
  5. Cook for 10 minutes until the chicken is thoroughly cooked.
  6. Add the fish sauce, salt, sugar, red chillies, citrus leaves, basil and stir it for a few seconds over the heat to warm them through.
  7. Turn the heat off and serve it warm with hot rice or rice noodle.
